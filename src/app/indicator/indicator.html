Indicator Book
Entity: IndicatorBook — Serial numbering for letters, preventing duplicates across fiscal years
📒 Why is the IndicatorBook critical?
Every letter must carry a globally unique reference number. This number is typically a compound of fiscal year + sequential serial. The IndicatorBook guarantees no two letters within a year share the same number. At the start of each new year the sequence resets to 1 — prior years are sealed and archived.

⚙ Numbering Business Rules
✅ Allowed
Each year resets from 1. 2025/0001 and 2026/0001 can coexist — they are distinct composite keys.
❌ Forbidden
Two letters with the same number in the same year. E.g., two letters bearing 2026/4021 is a constraint violation.
⚠ Implementation Note
The sequence increment must be atomic. In concurrent environments use a DB Sequence or row-level lock to prevent race conditions.
