📒 Why is the IndicatorBook critical?
Every letter must carry a globally unique reference number. This number is a compound of fiscal year + type + sequential serial. Each Typecorrespondence (Incoming, Outgoing, Internal) maintains its own independent counter per year — so 2026/Incoming/0041 and 2026/Outgoing/0041 are distinct. At the start of each new year all sequences reset to 1; prior years are sealed and archived.

⚙ Numbering Business Rules
✅ Allowed

Each year + type resets from 1. 2025/Incoming/0001 and 2026/Incoming/0001 can coexist — they are distinct composite keys.

❌ Forbidden

Two letters of the same type bearing the same number in the same year. E.g., two letters 2026/Outgoing/0041 is a constraint violation.

⚠ Implementation Note

Sequence increment must be atomic. Use a DB Sequence or RowVersion optimistic concurrency to prevent race conditions in concurrent environments.
